# Visual Storyboard

## Executive Deck

Purpose:

Define the visual language and storytelling style for the executive deck.

This document controls:

Visual identity

Slide composition

Story flow

Emotional progression

Presentation feel

Rule:

Do not optimize for decoration.

Optimize for executive clarity.

---

---

# Design Principles

Visual Style:

Premium

Modern

Minimal

Technical

Warm

Professional

Personality:

Trusted

Pragmatic

Visionary

Fast

Bold

Avoid:

Corporate stock style

Consulting overload

Technical complexity

Overuse of icons

Overuse of text

Prefer:

Whitespace

Strong hierarchy

Visual metaphors

Simple diagrams

Executive readability

---

Primary Feel:

Energy

Clean

Professional

Avoid:

Heavy gradients

# Typography Direction

---

# Slide Quality Checklist

Every slide should answer:

What is the one message?

Can it be understood in 5 seconds?

Would an executive forward it?

Does it feel premium?

Would this still work if spoken without slides?

---

# Final Feeling

Audience should leave thinking:

“These people understand transformation and have a practical path to help us.”
