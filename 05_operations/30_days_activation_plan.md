# 30 Day Activation Plan

## Company Activation Sprint

Status:
Active

Owner:
Founder

Purpose:

Move from company design to company validation.

Goal:

Create evidence.

Not assets.

Success Definition:

Real customer conversations.

Real signals.

Real learning.

Duration:

30 Days

---

# Month Objective

Validate:

Positioning

Offer resonance

Customer problems

Use case demand

Commercial assumptions

Primary Output:

Validated company direction.

Secondary Output:

Customer opportunities.

---

# Operating Principle

Build less.

Learn faster.

Do not optimize for scale.

Optimize for evidence.

---

# Week 1 — Activate Assets

Objective:

Prepare customer-facing execution.

Tasks:

Review repository.

Review customer assets.

Review delivery workflow.

Prepare outreach messaging.

Create target account list.

Success Criteria:

Company assets usable.

Outputs:

Outreach list.

Readiness review.

---

# Week 2 — Start Conversations

Objective:

Create learning.

Tasks:

Identify:

20 target organizations.

Reach out.

Book conversations.

Run discovery.

Capture signals.

Target:

5 conversations.

Questions:

What resonates?

What confuses?

What excites?

Success Criteria:

Signals collected.

Outputs:

Conversation notes.

---

# Week 3 — Run Validation

Objective:

Stress-test assumptions.

Tasks:

Run workshop format.

Run scoring.

Test offers.

Track objections.

Target:

5 additional conversations.

Success Criteria:

Patterns emerge.

Outputs:

Evidence.

---

# Week 4 — Convert Learning

Objective:

Update Company OS.

Tasks:

Review validation engine.

Update assumptions.

Update positioning.

Adjust offers.

Prioritize next actions.

Success Criteria:

Evidence-based changes.

Outputs:

Company update.
